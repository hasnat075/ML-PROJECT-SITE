About DataSet

This dataset contains the information about the types of Drugs being used by the Consumers. The dataset contains the age of the consumers and their sex categorization, Blood Pressure Levels, Cholesterol Levels and the Ratio of Potassium Ration. The target feature is:

  • Drup Type
  • Sex
  • Blood Pressure Levels (BP)
  • Cholestrol Level
  • NA to Potassium Ratio
The main problem here in not just the feature sets and target sets but also the approach that is taken in solving these types of problems as a beginner.
